Sensory-, Stimuli-, and Snoezel Room with Therapy Light by LightCare

Light plays an essential role in a sensory room. Light therapy resets our inner clock/ circadian rhythm. Messing up our circadian rhythm can lead to many different illnesses/ diseases e.g. winter depression (SAD). The Sensory- Snoezel room uses light, audio, video and smell to help calm and heal the elder people.

Technical details

For this projcet I created an external, user friendly and intuitive, interface which worked in conjunction with LightCare OS. I was also in charge of QA testing the software updates of LightCare OS as well as overseeing the setup and quality of the Stimuli- Snoezel room in Billund and Grindsted.
